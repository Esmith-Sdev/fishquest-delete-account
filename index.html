Delete Your FishQuest Account

Users can delete their account directly in the FishQuest app by going to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. If you no longer have access to the app, email fishquestmobile@gmail.com with the subject “Delete FishQuest Account” and include your account email/username.

Please include the email or username associated with your FishQuest account so we can identify the account.

When your account is deleted, we will remove your profile, fishing logs, uploaded images, rig presets, challenge progress, and related app data.

Some data may be retained if required for security, legal, or fraud-prevention purposes.
